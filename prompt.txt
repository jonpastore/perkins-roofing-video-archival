HARD-WON LAWS — violating these is exactly how yesterday went wrong:
- Token presence is NOT claim support. "Tim recommends replacing shingles every 10 years" is
  100% his vocabulary and 100% invented. Vocabulary-overlap has a low precision ceiling BY
  CONSTRUCTION — no tuning fixes it.
- A noisy detector must NEVER drive automated LLM edits. A guard I shipped told the reviser to
  strip 'Costs', 'Risk', 'Value'; articles got worse, timed out at 30 min, and every check
  reported success. Report-only now.
- CONSULT THE COUNCIL BEFORE BUILDING: mcp__chatgpt5__gpt5_chat and
  mcp__grok-4__chat_completion, independently, "be blunt, what am I not seeing". In ~90 seconds
  they killed a shipped guard AND my planned fix for it AND my replacement design. Jon had to
  ask why I hadn't.
- NEVER game a metric. Candidates that "fix" density are 'in', 'new', 'season' — that is
  gaming. Never invent an item count ("8 Signs") unless the article really is a listicle.
- A deterministic fix can satisfy its checker while destroying meaning: two title trimmers cut
  mid-clause; a de-repeater rewrote the keyword inside <img alt>; my guard's _normalise deleted
  hyphens and called "L-flashing" a hallucination when Tim says "L flashings". RE-SCORE ALL
  CHECKS AFTER ANY FIX AND READ THE OUTPUT. Dry-run every content mutation.
- Verify the real call path before calling anything broken. I was wrong out loud 5+ times
  yesterday (passed db=None then declared a critic blind; called the real product 'Polyblast' a
  hallucination — Tim says it, video WK6ufUjnicc t=57; called routes unregistered when FastAPI
  0.139's include_router is merely lazy). Every one died to checking, none to reasoning.

ENVIRONMENT GOTCHAS (each cost real time):
- unset GOOGLE_APPLICATION_CREDENTIALS — .env points it at infra/vertex-dev-sa.json, which does
  NOT exist, and it overrides working ADC.
- articles is RLS-protected: session.info["tenant_id"] = 1 or every query errors with
  'unrecognized configuration parameter "app.tenant_id"'. Only tenant 1 exists.
- ALTER TABLE articles needs ACCESS EXCLUSIVE; regen holds a transaction open across its ~12-min
  LLM call. A queued ALTER blocks every later query, and killing the client does NOT kill the
  server-side statement (use pg_cancel_backend). Apply DDL only when regen is stopped.
- pgrep -f matches this shell's OWN wrapper. Use ps -eo cmd | grep "[r]egen..." | wc -l.
- WP REST needs WP_APP_PWD from Secret Manager; .env WP_PWD is the WEB login and 401s on REST
  BY DESIGN. Only ONE WP site exists (1205166.us6.myftpupload.com); perkinsroofing.com is on
  AWS and unreachable from this repo.
- No cloud-sql-proxy binary by default; download v2.14.1 (recipe in the continuation).
- AUDIT_ENABLED=0 under pytest: audit writes open a second connection so rows survive a
  rollback — right on Postgres, but SQLite is single-writer and waits out a lock, which turned
